# Bare-Body Recovery Instruction

**Draft recovery instruction - not legal advice, not immigration advice, not a substitute for emergency services.**

Bare-Body Recovery is the ultimate human test of the protocol.

A person may lose every interface: passport, phone, computer, bank card, paper package, login, device, money, domain and local institutional help.

The person should not become a legal zero merely because every interface was lost.

## What Bare-Body Recovery means

Bare-Body Recovery is the right to initiate recovery.

It is not the right to be automatically believed.

It is not a passport, asylum procedure, bank procedure, court order, platform command or automatic asset release.

## Recovery path

A person may begin by:

1. contacting a relay agent, legal aid actor, notary, archive, professional association, publisher, foundation or other trusted institutional route;
2. providing a memorized recovery phrase or other non-public clue only through a safe process;
3. locating public event handles or witness-commons records;
4. requesting a protected pause for high-risk actions;
5. identifying material anchors stored outside the failed jurisdiction;
6. asking validators to check formal sufficiency;
7. escalating to class-separated review and legal process if transfer is requested.

## Mnemonic caution

A mnemonic phrase may open a recovery conversation.

It must not by itself release archives, transfer ownership, change payee, activate succession or override law.

## Failure mode

If all anchors, records, witnesses, event handles, material packages and institutional traces are lost, recovery may be impossible.

PCP does not promise magic recovery from total erasure.

## Core rule

Recovery may begin with memory.

Transfer must not.
