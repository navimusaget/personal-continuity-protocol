## Requested notarial/witness act

The witness/notary is asked to certify only the following limited facts, if permitted by law:

1. the identity or declared identity of the depositor, as applicable;
2. the date and time of deposit;
3. the existence of the deposited file/package at that time;
4. the hash of the deposited file/package;
5. any signature verified at the time of deposit;
6. the physical or digital medium received;
7. the chain-of-custody note for the sealed package, if applicable.

## Not requested

The witness/notary is not asked to certify:

- the truth of every claim inside the package;
- inheritance rights;
- medical status;
- beneficial ownership;
- tax status;
- absence of dispute;
- legal validity in all jurisdictions;
- personal continuity beyond the limited act witnessed.

## Purpose

This deposit is intended to create an offline/human-readable anchor for future continuity review, court filing, platform notice, succession review, emergency maintenance, payee dispute or recovery process.

A notarial record is an anchor. It is not the owner of continuity.
