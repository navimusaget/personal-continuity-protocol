## Independence questions

- Are any anchors controlled by the same institution?
- Are any anchors controlled by the same family conflict?
- Are any anchors under the same regulator or coercive power?
- Are any anchors dependent on the same domain, email or account?
- Are any anchors only copies of the same failed interface?
- Could one adversary capture all anchors at once?

## Quorum note

For high-risk transfer, do not rely on one class.

Three confirmations from the same power chain are weaker than three confirmations from genuinely different classes.

## Core rule

Human weakness cannot be removed. It must be distributed.
